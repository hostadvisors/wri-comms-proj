### Banyan Posts

Banyan posts are best for encouraging ongoing dialogue, fostering a sense of community, and helping bridge digital and geographical gaps when uniting WRI as one global organization.

<Tabs>
<TabItem label="When to Use">
**Ideal Banyan scenarios:**
- Building ongoing conversations around strategic topics and initiatives
- Sharing visual content (photos, infographics, videos) that benefits from community engagement
- Encouraging staff engagement through comments, reactions, and discussions
- Creating searchable reference content that teams can return to over time
</TabItem>
<TabItem label="Best Practices">
**Banyan engagement strategies:**
- Encourage staff to become active content owners and contributors
- Ask engaging, specific questions to prompt meaningful discussion and participation
- Regularly audit and update content to ensure relevance and accuracy
- Cross-reference with Editorial Board announcements for consistency and coordination
- Use tags and categories strategically to improve discoverability
</TabItem>
</Tabs>
